Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a beautiful country in South Asia, bordered by India on three sides, Myanmar to the southeast, and the Bay of Bengal to the south. Its capital city is Dhaka, which is also the largest and busiest city. The country uses Bangladeshi Taka (BDT) as its currency, and the official language is Bangla. With a population of more than 170 million, it is one of the most densely populated countries in the world. The national flag of Bangladesh is dark green with a red circle, symbolizing sacrifice and greenery. Bangladesh achieved independence in 1971 after a nine-month Liberation War against Pakistan, and the Father of the Nation, Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, is remembered with great respect. The national flower is the shapla (water lily), the national bird is the doyel, and the national animal is the Royal Bengal Tiger.
